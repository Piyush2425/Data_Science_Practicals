Histogram of Fare (Required)¶

In [48]:
sns.histplot(dataset['fare'], bins=10, kde=False)
plt.title("Fare Distribution")
plt.show()
# Insight: Most passengers paid low fares, while only a few paid very high ticket prices.

Distribution Plot¶

In [49]:
sns.histplot(dataset['age'], bins=10, kde=True)
plt.title("Age Distribution")
plt.show()
# Insight: Most passengers were young adults, with fewer very young children and elderly passengers.

Joint Plot¶

In [50]:
sns.jointplot(x='age', y='fare', data=dataset, kind='scatter')
plt.show()
# Insight: Higher fares appear across adult ages, while many low fares are spread across all ages.

Rug Plot¶

In [51]:
